Project: GIS-Based Intra-City Logistics Optimization System

There is a need for more research to maximize GIS capabilities in tackling complex logistics, enhancing supply chain optimization, route planning, and real-time decision making.

Inefficient routing in urban environments leads to higher costs and losses due to delays and inconsistencies in delivery services, requiring adaptable, future-proof solutions.

General and specific objectives

The main aim of this project is to optimize logistics networks using GIS, enhancing efficiency by improving network planning, route optimization, and decision-making processes.

The specific objectives include:

1. To develop and implement a real-time routing algorithm that dynamically adjusts routes based on daily customer orders.

2. To conduct a comparative analysis of the current routing system and newly developed real-time routing system.

More will be updated as we proceed with the project
